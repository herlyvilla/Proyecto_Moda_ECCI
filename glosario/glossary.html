Glossary

Key concepts related to Color Theory, Sustainability and Circular Economy.

English Spanish Definition
Color Theory Teoría del color Study of how colors interact and create visual effects.
Color Palette Paleta de colores A selected group of colors used in a design.
Hue Matiz The basic characteristic that identifies a color.
Saturation Saturación The intensity or purity of a color.
Contrast Contraste The visual difference between colors or elements.
Sustainability Sostenibilidad Meeting present needs without harming future generations.
Sustainable Development Desarrollo sostenible Development that balances economic, social and environmental goals.
Circular Economy Economía circular An economic model focused on reusing and recycling resources.
Recycling Reciclaje The process of converting waste into reusable materials.
Upcycling Suprareciclaje Creating higher-value products from discarded materials.
Renewable Energy Energía renovable Energy obtained from naturally replenished resources.
Carbon Footprint Huella de carbono The amount of greenhouse gases generated by an activity.
Waste Management Gestión de residuos The collection, treatment and disposal of waste materials.
Eco-design Ecodiseño Designing products with environmental impact in mind.
Green Economy Economía verde An economy that promotes sustainability and environmental protection.
